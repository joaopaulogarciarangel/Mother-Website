# Mother-Website

Portfolio website I built for my mother, Maria Marta Garcia, an editorial proofreader, copy editor and translator with 14 years of experience.

**Live site:** https://joaopaulogarciarangel.github.io/Mother-Website/

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS with GSAP and ScrollTrigger. Kinetic typography, scroll-driven storytelling and a horizontal bookshelf showcasing 32 real published books she worked on. The commit history tells the story of the development, from the first drafts and design explorations to the final version deployed from `docs/`.
